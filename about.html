About Us

A small team that does things properly

Aspect Conveyancing is Lindy Harris and Jeanette, two experienced conveyancers who handle every transaction personally. You deal with the same people from start to finish.

Our Story

Started because the process shouldn't be this confusing

Before starting Aspect, Lindy spent years watching the same thing happen: people would sign contracts they didn't understand, get updates that made no sense, and spend weeks feeling anxious about a process that should have been straightforward. She started Aspect in 2017 because she knew it could be done better.

The work is the same (contracts, searches, settlements, compliance) but the way we communicate is not. We tell you what's happening, why it matters, and what comes next. In plain English.

It's a simple idea, but it makes a real difference when you're buying or selling a home. Especially if it's your first time.

Conveyancing is full of terminology that means nothing to most people. We don't assume you know what a "Section 7 search" is or why your settlement got delayed. We explain it clearly, and as many times as you need.

We're actually available

When you call, you get Lindy or Jeanette. Not a receptionist, not a voicemail tree, not a "we'll get back to you within 48 hours" autoresponder. We answer because we know that waiting for information when you're buying a house is stressful.
