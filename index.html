How It Works

Four steps from enquiry to settlement

Most of our clients are surprised by how straightforward the process is once someone's handling it for them. Here's what working with us looks like.

1

Get in touch

Call, email, or fill out the form. Tell us whether you're buying, selling, or transferring, and we'll let you know how we can help.

2

We review your contract

We go through the contract of sale, flag anything you should know about, and explain it all in plain English. No surprises.

3

We handle the paperwork

Title searches, council checks, liaising with the other party's conveyancer, bank coordination. We take care of all of it.

4

Settlement day

Everything's been checked, signed, and prepared. Settlement goes through, and you get the keys. We'll keep you updated the whole way.
